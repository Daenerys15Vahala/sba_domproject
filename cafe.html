About Us


At Fratushka, every cup tells a story. Our café was created as a welcoming space where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee meets great conversation, local art, and unforgettable live music.

We carefully prepare each drink using quality ingredients, offering everything from rich espresso and creamy lattes to handcrafted seasonal specialties. Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee, catching up with friends, or finding a quiet corner to study, Fratushka is designed to feel like a second home.

What makes us truly special are our Live Music Nights. On selected days each month, talented local musicians transform our café into an intimate venue filled with acoustic performances, jazz, indie, and more. These evenings are exclusive to our members, who can reserve their seats in advance to enjoy the music in a relaxed and cozy atmosphere.

Join us for a cup of coffee, stay for the experience, and become part of the Fratushka community.
